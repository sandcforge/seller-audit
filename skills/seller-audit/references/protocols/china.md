# China Connection Protocol

**When to read:** During the investigation phase (Section 2.5) when you encounter signals that suggest a seller may be operating from or routing inventory through China. Use this protocol to systematically identify high-risk patterns.

---

## 2.7.1 China Connection Protocol

- Pinyin name/email + generic products = HIGH RISK
- Check for: Chinese text, grey/black poly mailers, Yanwen/SF Express labels, "Ships from China", Shenzhen/Guangdong addresses, +86 phone numbers, "Returns to China warehouse"
